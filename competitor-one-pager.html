Where Plume Nexus Wins

  • AI Reports chatbot — “how did Tess do on Saturdays last quarter?” answered in seconds. None of the three ship this.
  • Walk-in + appointment in one tool — turn-rotation kiosk bridges what GG, Vagaro & Boulevard split or skip.
  • Full HR & payroll included — 1099 generation, compensation modeling, Gusto bridge — not a paid add-on.
  • Processor-agnostic on Stripe — own your fees, your data, your terms instead of vendor lock-in.
  • One communications inbox — SMS, email and in-app chat threaded per client.

Honest Trade-offs

  • No marketplace — Vagaro can drive discovery; we won't. If a salon lives off Vagaro leads, switching has a cost.
  • Memberships & packages — Vagaro and Boulevard have a decade of depth; ours covers the essentials.
  • Client-portal polish — Boulevard & GG are ahead on self-service finish; we're closing fast.
  • No dedicated CSM — Boulevard gives a human account manager; we're self-service by design.

Plume Nexus gives you Boulevard-class depth — plus AI reports, walk-in rotation, and built-in HR none of them ship — at GG-ish pricing, and you keep your own payment processor.
