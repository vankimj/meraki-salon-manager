# PT / fitness competitor landscape + pricing (June 2026)

Real pricing pulled from vendor sites for the personal-training vertical
go-to-market. **Two segments, opposite pricing logic.**

## Online coaching tools — bill PER ACTIVE CLIENT
Cheap to start, climb as you grow; native client apps + big exercise libraries;
all have free trials.
- **Trainerize:** Free(1) → $9(2) → Pro $23–275 (5–200) → Studio Plus $248/location (500–1000); 30-day trial; AI workout builder; add-ons (branded app $169 one-time, Stripe $10).
- **TrueCoach:** $26(5) / $58(20) / $137(50); 14-day trial; 3,000+ exercise videos; Stripe payments included; free for clients.
- **Everfit:** Free(5) → Pro $16–255 (5–300) → Studio $88–430 (50–500); per-client $0.81–3.17.
- **PT Enhance:** $67(10 clients) / $97(50) / $147(100+, 2 trainers); 14-day trial; since 2007; 3,000+ video lib, C.H.E.K education, online shop; **web app, not native.**

## Studio / gym ops platforms — per-location/staff or flat, expensive, mostly demo-only
- **Vagaro:** ~$24/mo+ **per bookable staff** (reg $30); 2.2% processing; free trial; **beauty + wellness + fitness — the multi-vertical model closest to us.** THE MOST DANGEROUS competitor (cheap + multi-vertical + established). Not a workout-program builder; no affiliate income.
- **BuzzOps:** $197/$349/$549 + add-ons $99–197 ea → $350–850 loaded; **NO trial** (demo + 30-day money-back); flat/unlimited staff; operator-built (90+ studios); CRM/lead-gen, native apps. (Its "affiliate/referral" is a $197/mo add-on = client *referrals*, NOT gear income.)
- **Mindbody:** $99–159 / $259–279 / $499–699 **per location** + **~20% marketplace commission** on top of 3.5% processing (= 23.5%); demo only; entrenched + widely resented.
- **Enhance Tech:** quote only; multi-location gyms; AI builder, native apps, gamification; newer.

## Strategic read
Can't out-price Vagaro at entry. **Lead on:** flat + unlimited clients + affiliate
income + Venmo + one-tool-multi-vertical + intake/waivers — **NOT "more features"**
(coaching apps out-feature us on programming; Vagaro out-prices entry).

**Honest gaps to close (priority):** (1) native client app, (2) exercise/video
library, (3) AI program-gen, (4) CRM/lead pipeline. Free trial = table stakes.

## Draft Plume Nexus pricing (PROPOSED, not decided)
Flat, unlimited clients, ~2 months free annual: **Solo $29 / Studio $59 / Pro $129.**
Undercuts ops platforms 3–7×, beats coaching apps at scale (no per-client climb),
competitive with a 2–3-staff Vagaro while flat. NOTE: live platform tiers are
currently $19/$49/$149 (`src/lib/planEntitlements.js`, shared across ALL verticals
incl. salon) — changing prices touches every tenant. Sell sheet currently shows
pricing as TBD on purpose.
